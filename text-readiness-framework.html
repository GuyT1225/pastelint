The Text Readiness Framework

Most tools focus on generating text. Real-world communication requires preparation.

The pipeline

  1. Generation — AI or human-created text.
  2. Cleanup — Remove formatting issues, artifacts, and noise.
  3. Revision — Improve clarity, tone, rhythm, and flow.
  4. Narration Readiness — Prepare text for speech and audio delivery.
  5. Accessibility Review — Improve readability and comprehension.
  6. Publishing — Deliver text to readers, listeners, or customers.

Why readiness matters

Generated text is not automatically ready for publication. Formatting artifacts, weak phrasing, readability issues, narration problems, and accessibility barriers often remain.

Text readiness is the process of preparing content for real-world use.

How PasteLint fits

  • PasteLint Clean → Cleanup
  • SecondDraft → Revision
  • SSML Builder → Narration Readiness
