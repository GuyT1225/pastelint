Text Preparation Journal suggestions

For Text Preparation Journal suggestions, send a source link and a short note about the cleanup problem it shows. You can open a GitHub issue and include the source link, a short summary, and why the text-prep problem matters.

Please do not send confidential, sensitive, client, personal, medical, legal, or private text. Summaries and anonymized examples are best.
