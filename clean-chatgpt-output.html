    How PasteLint differs from AI humanizers

    PasteLint is not an AI detector bypass tool, and it does not try to disguise how text was written.

    Instead, PasteLint focuses on practical cleanup: readability, formatting, spacing, structure, and clarity.

    The goal is simple: help people review and improve text before they publish, share, narrate, or reuse it.

    • Preserves meaning instead of rewriting everything.
    • Highlights issues instead of hiding them.
    • Runs locally in your browser.
    • No login. No upload. No server processing.

    Private by design

    PasteLint runs in your browser. Your text is not uploaded to a server, stored in an account, or sent to an API.
