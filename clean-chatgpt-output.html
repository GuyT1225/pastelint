Trust note

Not an AI humanizer

PasteLint is not an AI detector bypass tool, and it does not try to disguise how text was written. It focuses on practical cleanup: readability, formatting, spacing, structure, and clarity.

The goal is to help people review and improve text before they publish, share, narrate, or reuse it.
